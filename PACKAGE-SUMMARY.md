### **1. Anti-Gravity Effect**

**Code:** `.anti-gravity-card { animation: float 8s ... infinite; }`

**Impact:** Users notice movement, stay longer, feel engaged

### **2. Parallax Scrolling**
Background glow elements move slower than foreground, creating illusion of depth.

**Code:** GSAP ScrollTrigger with scrub parameter

**Impact:** Modern, immersive experience

### **3. Magnetic Buttons**
Buttons subtly follow cursor, creating interactive "pull" effect.
