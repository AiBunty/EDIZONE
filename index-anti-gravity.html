16 Months Before You Fly

Your step-by-step roadmap to studying abroad – from research to arrival at your dream university.

Months 16-15

Research & Self-Assessment

Identify goals, take GPaths Assessment, research universities

Months 14-12

Exam Preparation

Begin IELTS/TOEFL/SAT training with focused study schedules

Months 11-10

Exam Attempts

Take entrance exams, aim for target scores in first or second attempts

Months 9-8

Application Preparation
