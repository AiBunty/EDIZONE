Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about study abroad, applications, visas, and career guidance.

What is the GPaths Assessment and how does it help? +
GPaths Assessment is an INACE-certified, scientifically-backed framework that evaluates your aptitudes, interests, personality, learning preferences, and career motivators. It provides a comprehensive Future-Ready Career Report covering both traditional and emerging fields, helping you make informed decisions about your career and study abroad journey.
Which countries and universities do you specialize in? +
We specialize in top universities across USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, New Zealand, Europe, and Singapore. We have partnerships with 500+ ranked universities globally and can guide you to institutions matching your academic profile, career goals, and budget requirements.
How does the application process work? +
Our end-to-end process spans 16 months: Research & self-assessment (months 16-15), exam preparation (14-12), exam attempts (11-10), application preparation (9-8), application submission (7-6), admission & financial planning (5-4), visa processing (3-2), and arrival & pre-departure (1-0). We guide you through every step with personalized support.
Do you offer scholarship and financial aid assistance? +
Yes! We provide comprehensive financial planning including identification of scholarship opportunities with our university partners. We also assist with education loan applications and connect you with financial resources to make quality education affordable. Our goal is to help you find the best financial options for your situation.
What visa support do you provide? +
We provide complete visa processing support including document preparation, application guidance, interview coaching, and tracking. Our high visa success rate reflects our expertise in handling requirements across USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, and other destinations. We guide you from application submission to approval.
